Things I like

Audio Equipment

  • Speakers
    • Single point source

      • I have a fasination with simple design. A single point source speaker uses
      a single driver to cover all of the frequencies. The reason that these are
      interesting is that they need no crossovers which many believe to tarnish the sound.

  • Amplifiers
    • Vintage tube amplifiers

      • I currently own a Dynco ST-70 tube amplifier. It is incredible sounding and can get
      louder than my apartment will allow for. I like tube amplifiers because they
      can reproduce sounds in a very smooth way; whereas they don't color the sounds
      or make music harsh.

Vintage Vehicles

  • Cars
    • VW

      • I own a 1980 VW Rabbit that I am in the process of rebuilding and restoring. It's a
      long process but it is entertaining to me. There is a sense of zen I get from
      slowly piecing something mechanical back together for use.

  • Motorcycles
    • Triumph

      • I used to have a more modern Triumph that I managed to put almost 10,000 miles on in
      about a year and a half. I rode it almost every day. I also really enjoy the
      old Triumphs from the 60's and 70's. The way they run is something that will
      never exist again.

    • Yamaha XS650

      • I would like to one day own a 70's Yamaha XS650. Yamaha basically decided to make a Triumph
      but one that ran all of the time which Triumphs were not known to do.
